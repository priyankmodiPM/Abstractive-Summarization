hundreds of survivalists and so-called ' preppers ' descended on the salt lake city , utah , suburb of sandy on friday for the first ever preppercon .
during the two-day expo , they were shown specially-equipped underground bunkers , learned new methods of storing food , and even dressed up as zombies .
they were also greeted by actors from the amc hit show ' the walking dead ' , including addy miller , who took pictures with fans and acted as a judge in an emergency preparedness food cook-off .
