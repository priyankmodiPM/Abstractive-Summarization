west ham have announced a new five-year multi-million pound kit deal with umbro .
umbro produced the first-ever west ham replica kits for sale in the 1960s when the likes of bobby moore , sir geoff hurst and martin peters wore the claret and blue in their 1964 fa cup and 1965 european cup winners ' cup triumphs .
umbro supplied the kit for the great west ham team of the sixties .
