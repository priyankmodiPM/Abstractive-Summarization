ryanair has taken the unprecedented step of banning passengers from bringing their own booze on board flights between glasgow and the party island of ibiza .
the dublin-based airline has informed passengers travelling on the notorious flight from glasgow prestwick airport to the spanish island that their alcohol purchases from duty free shops are no longer allowed in the cabin .
ryanair refused to explain the reason behind its decision , but it follows a handful of incidents involving boorish behaviour by inebriated travellers .
