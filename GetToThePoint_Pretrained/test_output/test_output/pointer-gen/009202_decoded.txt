manny pacquiao has become one of the most recognisable stars in the world of sport after a series of mega-fights .
the filipino has been involved in some of the biggest bouts in the world over the past decade or so , facing the likes of oscar de la hoya , ricky hatton , miguel cotto and four fights against mexican warrior juan manuel marquez .
pacquiao has made a remarkable rise through the weight divisions from being an incredibly slight light-flyweight when he made his debut .
