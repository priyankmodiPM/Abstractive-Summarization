barry hawkins kept his nerve to clinch a final frame decider over matthew selt in the first round of the world snooker championship .
the 2013 finalist looked set to ease through to the last 16 after resuming on monday with a 7-2 overnight lead .
selt responded by reeling off five frames in a row - including back-to-back centuries - before a cool clearance from hawkins saw him into the second round with a 10-9 win .
