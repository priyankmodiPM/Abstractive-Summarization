a new lighter uses electricity to ignite , and it’s claimed to be the first flameless gadget of its kind .
the device , called the illume arclighter , creates a ‘ super high-intensity’ electrical arc and contains a rechargeable lithium-ion battery so users will never run out of fuel at a tricky moment again .
instead of using a spark and fuel mechanism like traditional lighters , the illume arclighter creates a super high-intensity’ electrical arc between two ceramic electrodes .
