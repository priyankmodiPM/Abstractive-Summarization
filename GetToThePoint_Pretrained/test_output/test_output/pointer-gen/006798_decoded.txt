11 former atlanta public school educators were convicted wednesday of racketeering for their role in a scheme to inflate students ' scores on standardized exams .
the educators fed answers to students or erased and changed the answers on tests after they were turned in to secure promotions or up to $ 5,000 each in bonuses .
they were all found guilty under the racketeer influenced and corrupt organizations act , or rico , which is typically reserved for major mobsters .
