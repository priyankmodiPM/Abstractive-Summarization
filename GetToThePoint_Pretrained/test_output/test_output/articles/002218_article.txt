' since he arrived there has been a clear upwards trend , ' midfielder vicente iborra said of emery , who joined sevilla in january 2013 , on the club 's radio station on wednesday . ' those who know him know that he lives for football and for his club , with all the responsibility that brings . ' and the important thing is that it rubs off on us . having a coach and training staff like that is to be valued , it 's not easy and we have to , and will , make the most of it .
