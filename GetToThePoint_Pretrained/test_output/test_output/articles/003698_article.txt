england will send an under 20 team to the toulon tournament this summer following the successful experiment last year . gareth southgate’s squad finished fourth last may and the nucleus of that group will be involved in the european under 21 championships this june . the fa felt the diversity and quality of opposition in toulon were key to helping young players’ education and the decision has been made to enter once again . aidy boothroyd will lead the team this time in the toulon tournament that runs from may 27 until june 7 . gareth southgate ( right ) led england to fourth in the toulon tournament last may . liverpool winger jordon ibe ( left ) could feature in the squad for the competition . aidy boothroyd will be the man in charge of the under 20s this time and his squad could include players such as liverpool’s jordon ibe . other participating countries include holland , the ivory coast , mexico and the united states . the tournament runs from may 27 to june 7 .
