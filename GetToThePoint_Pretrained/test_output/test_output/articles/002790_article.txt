sidney . spell pleaded guilty in october to killing sherry arnold , 43 , after attempting to abduct the popular math teacher as she was jogging near her home in 2012 . arnold 's body was found months later buried in a shallow grave in a rural area near williston , north dakota . co-defendant lester van waters jr. was sentenced in december to 80 years in prison on a charge of deliberate homicide by
