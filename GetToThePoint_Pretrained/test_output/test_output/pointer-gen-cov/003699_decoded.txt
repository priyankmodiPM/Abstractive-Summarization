donna christie , named by locals as donna christie , slipped and fell from hassans fall lookout at lithgow on tuesday afternoon .
she was bushwalking with a male friend and her two children , aged 12 and 13 , when she dropped her phone at the lookout , which is almost 1,100 metres above sea level .
her friend raised the alarm and it took two hours for police , paramedics and the lithgow volunteer rescue squad to rescue the woman .
