protests by japanese-american groups and star trek 's george takei have led an east coast auction house to cancel a sale of 450 photos and artifacts from world war ii internment camps .
the items from the collection of ' dean of american crafts ' allen eaton were slated for public auction on friday at the rago auction house in lambertville , new jersey .
the collection includes 63 photos of people of japanese descent who were imprisoned over fears they were spies and dozens of arts and crafts they made .
