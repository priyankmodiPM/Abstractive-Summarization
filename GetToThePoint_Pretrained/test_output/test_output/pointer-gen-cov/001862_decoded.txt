reanne evans saw her 10-year reign as women 's world snooker champion surprisingly ended on tuesday .
the 29-year-old from dudley earlier this month lost narrowly to ken doherty in the first round of qualifying for the crucible-staged betfred world championship .
she relinquished her wlbsa world ladies title , losing 4-2 in the semi-finals to hong kong 's ng on yee , who went on to defeat england 's emma bonney 6-2 in the final .
