sebastiano quagliata and his wife , valbona lucaj , pleaded no contest to owning a dangerous dog causing death in the fatal mauling of craig sytsma of livonia .
sytsma , 46 , was attacked by two cane corsos last july in metamora township , 45 miles northwest of detroit .
lucaj , a native of albania , and quagliata , of italy , could be deported after serving their sentences .
