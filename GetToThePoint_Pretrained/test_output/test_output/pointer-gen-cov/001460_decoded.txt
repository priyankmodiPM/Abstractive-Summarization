west brom have honoured the memory of former striker jeff astle on ' astle day ' against leicester city in saturday 's premier league encounter .
the baggies are commemorating the launch of the jeff astle foundation and are wearing a replica of their 1968 fa cup winning kit , where astle scored the winner against everton .
astle died of chronic traumatic encephalopathy in 2002 at the age of 59 .
west brom usually take to the field at the hawthorns in a white and blue kit .
