jack grealish wants to replace the wembley misery he suffered as a fan with joy as a player when aston villa face liverpool in the fa cup semi-final .
grealish , 19 , was handed his first premier league start by tim sherwood in the 3-3 draw with queens park rangers and is hopeful of retaining his place for the april 19 date .
grealish saw villa lose twice at wembley in 2010 - in the league cup final and in the fa cup semi-final .
