neil phillips , the team doctor for england 's victorious 1966 world cup campaign , has died at the age of 83 .
manager sir alf ramsey promoted phillips from his role as under-23 team doctor just before the '66 world cup as seniors doctor alan bass had run out of holiday .
phillips , who was born in tredegar in south wales , was england 's team doctor for the defence of the jules rimet trophy four years later in mexico and left the fa in 1974 .
