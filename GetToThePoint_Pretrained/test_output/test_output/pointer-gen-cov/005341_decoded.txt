city code enforcement officials in ogden , utah , have told resident jeremy trentelman to take down the elaborate box fort he built for his son and daughter in his front yard .
the family said it will leave the box fort which includes a slide and trampoline , up for 14 days , the maximum allowed before a fine .
trentelman was only planning on leaving the fort in his yard for a few days , but now has decided to leave it up for the maximum two weeks before he receives a $ 125 fine .
