david petraeus will not go to jail for giving his mistress classified material while she was working on a book about him , a judge ruled today .
the former cia director avoided jail time at the hearing in charlotte , north carolina on thursday and was instead sentenced to two years probation .
he was also fined $ 100,000 - more than double the $ 40,000 his attorneys had requested .
petraeus had agreed two months ago to plead guilty to a misdemeanor count of unauthorized removal and retention of classified material .
