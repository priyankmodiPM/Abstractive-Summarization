liverpool manager brendan rodgers has been convicted of leaving a property to rot .
the anfield boss was ordered to pay £400 and £375 costs , with a £40 surcharge at blackburn magistrates ' court for leaving a £69,950 terrace house in accrington with broken windows and doors and rubbish strewn across the garden .
rodgers - whose liverpool side crashed out of the fa cup on sunday in a 2-1 defeat by aston villa - were found guilty of ignoring an improvement notice issued by hyndburn council .
