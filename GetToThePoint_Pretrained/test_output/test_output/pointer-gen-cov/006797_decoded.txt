rare paintings used to plan the filming of iconic scenes in the wizard of oz have sold for £35,000 some 76 years after the film was first released .
one of the stunning watercolours , showing the moment dorothy , toto , the tinman , the lion and the scarecrow meet the wizard of oz for the first time , went for £25,000 .
the other , imagining the emerald city , capital of the land of oz and home to the wizard , sold for £10,000 .
