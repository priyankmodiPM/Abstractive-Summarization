mario ambarita , 21 , took chances with his life when he clambered into the wheel housing of the garuda indonesia flight which took off from the main island of sumatra .
he had climbed into the wheel housing when the aircraft stopped at the end of the runway in pekan baru , sumatra , during a scheduled touch down to pick up passengers .
ground staff stared in amazement as the young man started staggering away from the aircraft before he was grabbed by security officials .
