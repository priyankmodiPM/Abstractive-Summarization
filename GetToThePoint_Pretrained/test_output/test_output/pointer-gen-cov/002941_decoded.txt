former world champion ken doherty launched a spirited fightback to lead snooker 's top woman player reanne evans 5-4 at the interval of their world championship qualifier in sheffield .
doherty , who won the title in 1997 , won four of the final five frames of the morning session after trailing 3-1 at one stage against 29-year-old evans , a single mum from dudley who is bidding to become the first woman to appear in the main stages of the world finals at the crucible .
