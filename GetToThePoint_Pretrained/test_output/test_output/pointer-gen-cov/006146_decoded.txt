oregon-based defense contractor flir systems inc. has agreed to pay $ 9.5 million to settle bribery charges filed by the securities and exchange commission that involved expensive trips and gifts given to middle eastern government officials .
the commission said the thermal-imaging company earned more than $ 7 million in profits from sales influenced by the gifts .
the company also arranged travel for saudi officials , including a 20-night trip with stops in beirut , casablanca , dubai , new york and paris .
