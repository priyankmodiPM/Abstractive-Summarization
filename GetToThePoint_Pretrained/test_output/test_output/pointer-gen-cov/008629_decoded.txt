ap mccoy has confirmed he will ride to the final race at sandown on saturday if he can secure mounts .
there had been speculation that the soon-to-be-crowned 20-time champion jockey would end his career after riding in the ap mccoy celebration chase , in which he definitely partners mr mole , or the feature bet365 gold cup .
but there is now a good chance his final mount as a professional jockey will come in the handicap hurdle run at 5.35 .
