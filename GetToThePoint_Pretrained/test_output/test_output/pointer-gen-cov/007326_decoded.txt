alondra luna nunez was mistakenly flown to texas on april 16 after a judge in the western state of michoacan ruled in favor of a houston woman named dorotea garcia who believed she was her daughter .
she was returned to her family in mexico on wednesday after dna testing in the united states showed she was not the long-missing daughter of houston resident dorotea garcia .
