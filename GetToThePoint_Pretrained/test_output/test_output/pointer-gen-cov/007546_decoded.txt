the australian air transport safety bureau released its report on wednesday into the ' wheels up landing ' on december 12 , 2014 at jabiru airport , southeast of darwin .
the pilot was flying a six-seat cessna 310 aircraft , registered vh-tbe , with two adults and three children on board when he became distracted by one of the passengers .
the pilot also reported that the children on board were ' excited and a little disruptive ' during the flight .
