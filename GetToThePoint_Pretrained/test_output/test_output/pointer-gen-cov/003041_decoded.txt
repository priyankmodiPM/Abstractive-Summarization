dikembe mutombo , a congolese centre whose legendary finger-wagging motion became one of the most recognized gestures in the game , was a four-times nba defensive player of the year .
mutombo played in the nba from 1991 until 2009 , recording 11,729 points , 12,359 rebounds , and 3,289 blocks during 18 seasons .
