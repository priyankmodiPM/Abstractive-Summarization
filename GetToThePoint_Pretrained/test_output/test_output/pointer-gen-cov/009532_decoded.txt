everton forward steven naismith has won the barclays spirit of the game award for his selfless charity work and contributions to the local community .
the 28-year-old has worked with the likes of job centre plus , dyslexia scotland and the whitechapel centre in liverpool , to help give back to the communities that have supported him throughout his football career .
